LIFT AND EARN

LIFT AND EARN is an innovative fitness and rewards platform that motivates users to stay active while earning cryptocurrency. With our dedicated $LIFT token on the Solana blockchain, users can seamlessly earn rewards by participating in fitness activities. The platform integrates with a Telegram Mini-app, providing users with easy access to their progress, rewards, and community interactions.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

  • STEP 1

    Start by joining our Telegram Mini-app, where you'll find everything you need to get started, including tutorials, support, and community discussions. Sync your preferred fitness tracker or use our integrated tracking features to log your workouts and physical activities.

  • STEP 2

    Every step, workout, or fitness milestone you achieve earns you $LIFT tokens. The more active you are, the more you earn! Use the Telegram Mini-app to monitor your activity levels, view your earnings, and stay motivated with real-time updates and challenges.

  • STEP 3

    Exchange your $LIFT tokens for exclusive rewards, discounts on fitness gear, or even trade them on supported exchanges. The possibilities are endless! Connect with other fitness enthusiasts, share your progress, participate in challenges, and grow within our vibrant community.
